About Us

During the day, Amaya, Connor and Greg lead totally normal lives, but they transform into superheroes during the night and use their powers to fight villains and solve mysteries.



PJ Masks Save The Day Communities

PJ Masks Save The Day is a cross-platform PJ Masks communities, that aims to form a family-friendly communities by PJ Masks fan from each part of the world and the internet. A community of real PJ Masks fan that love the show and wanted to discuss about the show, character, episodes, season, location and etc on cross-platform across Revolt, Guilded, Facebook, Messenger and etc.
